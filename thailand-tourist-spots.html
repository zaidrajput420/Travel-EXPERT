Bangkok

Bangkok is the capital and most populous city of Thailand. It's a bustling metropolis with a rich cultural heritage and numerous attractions, including the Grand Palace, Wat Phra Kaew, Wat Arun, Chatuchak Weekend Market, and more.

Chiang Mai

Chiang Mai is a city in northern Thailand that's known for its ancient temples, scenic beauty, and cultural heritage. Some of the top attractions in Chiang Mai include Doi Suthep, Wat Phra Singh, Chiang Mai Night Bazaar, and more.

Phuket

Phuket is an island in southern Thailand that's famous for its stunning beaches, clear waters, and lively nightlife. Some of the top attractions in Phuket include Patong Beach, Kata Beach, Phi Phi Islands, Big Buddha, and more.
